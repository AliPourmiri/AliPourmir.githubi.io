About me

I am an assistant professor at CE department of University of Isfahan. Prior to joining UI, I was a postdoctoral fellow in CS school of IPM and I got my PhD from Max Planck Institute for Informatics, where I was working under supervision Kurt Mehlhorn and Thomas Sauerwald. Broadly speaking, I am interested in design and analysis of (randomized) algorithms, more specifically problems arisen in networks, such as information diffusion, random walks and load balancing on networks.
